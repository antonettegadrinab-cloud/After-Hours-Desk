Execution quality system

Stop guessing why you lost

The rubric forces you to evaluate every trade on 4 dimensions. Over time you'll see exactly where your execution breaks down — and fix it.

1
Followed Plan
Did you follow all checklist rules? Was it a clean, rule-based entry with no shortcuts?
2
Entry Accuracy
How precise was your entry? Did you enter exactly at the OB/FVG/key level or did you chase?
3
Timing
Were you early or late? Did you wait for full confirmation — CHOCH + displacement — before entering?
4
Emotional Control
Were you calm and patient? Or did fear, greed, or FOMO drive the decision?
Final score = A, B, C, or D
A
Professional execution
16–20 points · System is working
B
Decent, needs refinement
11–15 points · Minor improvements
C
Inconsistent execution
6–10 points · Review your process
D
Undisciplined trading
4–5 points · Full review required
